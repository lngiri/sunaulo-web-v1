Message From Chairman

Namaste ,Esteemed Visitors, 

Welcome to our digital abode, crafted under the banner of the Federation of Nepal Gold Silver Gem and Jewellery Associations. As the President, I am honored to extend a heartfelt invitation to explore this platform—a testament to our industry's unity ,transparency, and progress.
 
This website mirrors our commitment to growth, ethics, and innovation. Here, you'll find insights into our industry's pulse, be it market trends, regulatory updates, artisanal showcases, or educational resources. It's a hub for industry veterans, artisans, investors, and enthusiasts alike.
 
I'm indebted to all who've contributed to this endeavor, as it mirrors our industry's vibrancy. I urge you to dive in, learn, and collaborate, as together, we can elevate Nepal's gold, silver, gem, and jewellery sector globally.                       
                     
Warm regards,
Kishan Sunar
President
Federation of Nepal Gold Silver Gem and Jewellery Associations

About Us

Empowering Nepal's Precious Craftsmanship

The Federation of Nepal Gold Silver Gem & Jewellery Associations was established on Magh 26, 2069 BS(8 February 2013) by pioneering business persons in the gem and jewellery industry.
 
It is an authorized national-level organization representing individuals in the gem and jewellery business.The federation has been actively working as a facilitator for various tasks within the Gold, Silver, Gem, and Jewellery sector, while also addressing emerging challenges in the current landscape through unity.
